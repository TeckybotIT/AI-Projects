Traditional irrigation techniques such as flood irrigation and manual scheduling often lead to water wastage, uneven distribution, reduced crop yields, and soil degradation. Farmers also face challenges in determining the right crop for their soil type, predicting irrigation needs, and managing resources efficiently.

Our project uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to optimize irrigation by analyzing soil, crop, and weather data to provide real-time recommendations and predictions for efficient water usage and improved agricultural productivity.

What Our Project Can Do

  • Analyze soil and crop data for better decisions.
  • Provide irrigation scheduling based on weather.
  • Recommend the best crop for given soil.
  • Predict water requirements accurately.
  • Support farmers with AI-driven insights.

Benefits of Our Project

  • Reduces water wastage and increases efficiency.
  • Improves crop productivity and yield.
  • Decreases farming costs with optimal resources.
  • Ensures sustainable agriculture practices.
  • Helps farmers make data-driven decisions.
