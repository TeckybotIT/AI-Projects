About this Project

Soil classification plays a vital role in agriculture as it helps farmers identify the right type of soil for specific crops, improve productivity, and make better use of resources. Traditionally, soil identification is done manually, which is time-consuming, less accurate, and often requires expert knowledge.

To overcome these drawbacks, we designed a deep learning-based soil classification system that analyzes soil images and predicts the correct soil type. Our model is capable of identifying seven major Indian soils ‚Äî Alluvial, Arid, Black, Laterite, Mountain, Red, and Yellow ‚Äî and also suggests the most suitable crops for each soil type.

With a training accuracy of 91%, this project demonstrates how AI can effectively support farmers in making data-driven agricultural decisions.
